About Redd Swindells (AI2N)

Originally licensed in 1968, my primary ham radio interests are DXing, Education, and Public Service.

Retired at the end of 2007 as Chief Instrument & Control/Metronics Tech in the Fossil/Nuclear power industry.

Member of ARRL, Fulton (NY) ARC, Rochester (NY) DX Assoc., Iguana Air Corps Contest Club, Beer Scouts of America Troop 13126.

Achievements

  • 9BDXCC (86 on 6m), 9BWAS (47 on 6m)
  • Triple Play WAS, W1AW Centennial WAS, WAC, WAZ, VUCC (400-6m)
  • DXCC Challenge (2330+), WPX Mixed (2775+)
