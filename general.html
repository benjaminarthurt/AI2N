Information

  • Textbook: use the ARRL General Class License Manual --->10th Edition<--- Make sure to get the correct edition.
    • It’s $32.95 here - SPIRAL bound (Lays flat; I love it): https://home.arrl.org/action/Store/Product-Details/productId/2009476073
    • It’s also available on Amazon, for $29.66 and quicker delivery: https://www.amazon.com/dp/162595171X/
  • The ARRL has on-line exam review available at http://arrlexamreview.appspot.com/ There is no ARRL membership requirement to use the web-based review application, though you’ll have to establish an identity and password.
  • Alternatively, you can also take practice exams at https://www.aa9pw.com/ after creating a free account.
  • Taking practice tests on-line is the best way to discover your individual weaknesses, so they can be eliminated.
